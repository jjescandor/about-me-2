About Me

Who Is She?

Hi! I'm Tiara Brown, an aspiring web developer who broke into the study of coding around July 2021. I started off my studies using a book called Coding For Dummies , but I've since moved on to online resources and a coding bootcamp. My hopes is to become a kick-ass web developer within a year or less if possible using a mix of free and paid online resources and studying as hard as I possibly can.
I tend to spend my free time coding and following tutorials, then applying them to my own project(s).

Why is she leaving her previous field?

The field of journalism is a tricky one to be in. The perks of it is that you get to interact with humans on a daily basis, for some that isn't a perk I know, that was really the highlight of the job for me. But when it boiled down to it, the company that I worked for wasn't really focused on making sure I grew as an individual. I felt bogged down with work and limited on the amount of times that I was assisted by upper-management. I do not miss that part of the job, but helping others on a daily basis and inspiring them to reach for what they needed was the highlight of every single day that I clocked in. Now, I won't say that everyone that I worked with was a drag, there were some pretty helpful coworkers there that turned into life-long friends, but that wasn't enough to keep me there.
As a web developer I am able to still be the creative person that I am and make a new impact on the world.
